Delivery Tracker Setup Guide

Before you begin setup:

  1. Have a USB webcam and a PYNQ-Z2 Board ready.

  2. Setup and connect your PYNQ-Z2 board to your router by following the official tutorial here.

Finding your Device Registration Serial Number:

  1. Flip over your PYNQ-Z2 Board to find your serial number.

Setting up your PYNQ-Z2 Environment:

  1. Create an account with your PYNQ-Z2 serial number here.

  2. Download and extract the Jupyter notebooks files and the haarcascade files here.

  3. Connect to your PYNQ-Z2 Board and create a new directory to add your downloaded files to.

  4. Open your newly created folder and upload your extracted files.

  5. In your newly created folder, create a new folder named data and move both xml files haarcascade_frontalface_default.xml and haarcascade_upperbody.xml to this folder.

Configuring the Notebook file:

  1. After connecting to your PYNQ-Z2 board, open Event Detection.ipynb.

  2. In the cell under Step 1, enter your PYNQ-Z2 board's serial number and the URL of your Delivery tracker Django server.

Running the Notebook file:

After setting your serial number and server url, follow the steps and run each cell sequentially.

Stopping your webcam:

To stop running your webcam, interrupt the kernel by pressing the stop button.
